Thought

Back in 2021, when I made Dear Delhi, There's Something Wrong With You, I was introduced to the media theories of Marshal McLuhan. The line, the medium is the message, stayed with me.

Initially, I explored expressive typography through posters but later realised that code could be a great enabler for expressive typography. Over the past year, I have explored various techniques of manipulating letterforms over the web, which I shall write about later. For now, you can enjoy the pretty outcomes below.
