Making (Micro) Tools for Graphic Design

For

Graphic design students (2nd-3rd year undergraduate / 1st year graduate)

Logistics

In-person, 5-10 days (flexible)

Requirements

Laptop with a working internet connection for each student

What?

The workshop introduces ‘creative-coding’ to design students, enabling them to make their own creative ‘micro-tools’ in the future. Students are equipped with an ability to ‘think like’, ‘talk to’ and ‘make with’ computers, leading to a collective exhibition of tools that fulfil a certain creative need (defined by each student).
